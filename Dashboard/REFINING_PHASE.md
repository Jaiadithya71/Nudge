# 🎯 Your Goal

# 🧪 PHASE 1 — SOLO DOGFOODING (YOU AS THE USER)

---

## 🕐 Step 1: Morning (Start of Day)

Open your dashboard.

### Ask:

* Does the **context reflect reality?**

  * tasks correct?
  * calendar correct?

* Does the **insight feel accurate?**

  * or generic?

* Do the **nudges feel relevant?**

---

### If NOT:

Log:

```text
[ISSUE] Insight too generic / missing context
```

## 🕒 Step 2: During the Day (CRITICAL)

This is where your system becomes real.

---

### Use the system actively:

* Complete a task → log action
* Ignore a nudge → do nothing
* Acknowledge → click button

---

### Ask:

* Did the system **notice my behavior?**
* Did nudges **change based on my actions?**

---

👉 If nothing changes:

> ❌ system is not reactive enough
